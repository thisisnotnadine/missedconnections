☮︎ About ☮︎

Craigslist is a privately held American company operating a classified advertisements website with sections devoted to jobs, housing, for sale, items wanted, services, community service, gigs, résumés, and discussion forums. In 1995, Craig Newmark began the service as an email distribution list to friends, featuring local events in the San Francisco Bay Area. I guess you can call it Craig's List. It became a web-based service in 1996 and expanded into other classified categories.

A missed connection is a type of personal advertisement that arises after two people meet but are too shy or otherwise unable to exchange contact details.

This website works as a collective map for recent missed archives from San Francisco, California. Every entry is from an actual anonymous person, living in San Francisco. Topography is from an official map the area, edited and tweaked by the author.
